pub enum ControllerRef {
    Primary,
    Secondary,
    Tertiary,
    Indexed(u32),
}

Used to persistently identity a controller, either by internal id or by role. Note that roles can refer to different physical devices at different times, while the internal id will remain locked to a particular device.
